​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​Facts about Exams

  • You have max. three attempts for each exam
  • You are automatically registered for exams and reexams
  • If you do not show for an exam or a reexam, you will use one exam attempt
  • If you fall sick on the day of an exam, you must submit a medical certificate within four days to the study administration to not use an exam attempt
  • You will be assessed on an individual basis, also when the exam is based on a group project
  • Plagiarism and cheating on an exam is taken very seriously
  • Read the criteria for tests and rule​s for exams carefully. It pays off!
  • If you are late, you may only enter the exam room and participate exam if the exam invigilator gives permission.
  • If you are more than 10 minutes late you must go to the Study Administration.
  • Before the exam begins, the exam invigilator will give oral directions.

Rules during the exam

  • Smoking is not allowed!
  • You are not allowed to go outside.
  • When you need to go the toilet, you must use the toilet advised to you by the exam invigilator.
  • Mobile phones are not allowed. If you bring a mobile phone, you must be handed over to the exam invigilator.
  • You are not allowed to exchange information with or in any way be in contact with other examinees or people outside the examination room.
  • There must be complete silence the last 30 minutes of the exam. You are allowed to hand in your exam paper, pack your things and leave the room before the exam has ended.

Cheating in exam

  • If you receive or give another examinee help during the exam you will be suspended from the exam and have used 1 examination attempt.
  • If doubt is raised that you have received or given help, or have submitted an exam paper made by someone else it will be reported to VIA.
